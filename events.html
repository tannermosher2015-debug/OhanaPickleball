Ways to Celebrate

Bring your whole crew

No experience needed — our coaches get everyone playing fast. You bring the people; we bring the courts, paddles and aloha.

Corporate Events

Off-sites, client days and company celebrations on covered courts. Equipment provided, zero experience required — a workout, a laugh and real team energy.

Team Building

Get the whole team rallying. Our coaches run beginner-friendly sessions that have everyone playing, laughing and competing in minutes.

Private Parties

Birthdays, bachelor & bachelorette groups, family reunions and island getaways — reserve the courts and make the day your own.

Group Tournaments

Round-robins and bracket play across multiple courts, organized and run by our team — friendly competition with real medals and bragging rights.
