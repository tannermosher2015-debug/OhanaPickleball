Our Story

Built by a family,
for the ohana

OHANA wasn't built by a corporation. It was built by Pat, Jen and their sons Reid and Clay — a family that packed up, moved to Maui, and spent a year making something they believe in.

Pat Rolfes is a pickleball pioneer: he grew Newport Beach from four cracked courts to thirty-one, backed MLP and DUPR at the start, and founded The HUB across multiple cities. Maui is the personal one — built to help a community still recovering from the 2023 Lahaina fire.

Read our full story
Our mission has always been to build communities through pickleball. We now consider the Hawaiians and Tongans who helped build this family as well.
