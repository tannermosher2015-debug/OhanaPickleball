Our Story

Built by a family.
For the ohana.

OHANA wasn't built by a corporation. It was built by Pat, Jen and their sons Reid and Clay — a family that packed up, moved to Maui, and spent a year making something they believe in.

Pat Rolfes is a pickleball pioneer: he helped grow Newport Beach from four cracked courts to thirty-one, backed MLP and DUPR at the start, and founded The HUB across multiple cities. Maui is the personal one — built to help a community still recovering from the 2023 Lahaina fire.
